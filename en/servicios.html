Disciplines

Nine styles, one tribe

From Latin rhythms to classical ballet. Classes for kids, teens and adults, at every level.

Salsa

Cuban salsa and line salsa, plus rueda de casino for high-energy group dancing.

Bachata

The most sensual and popular partner dance. Rhythm, connection and musicality, step by step.

Kizomba

Elegance and closeness as a couple. Discover tarraxo too, its more intimate side.

Urban

Urban dance with style and attitude for all ages, with a competition group.

Ballet

The foundation of classical dance: technique, posture and elegance for children, juniors and adults.

Oriental dance

Expression, rhythm and hip movement. A dance that connects body and music.

Flamenco

Rhythm, arm movement and footwork. Flamenco art from early childhood classes to advanced levels.

Sevillanas

The quintessential dance of Spain's ferias (festivals). Learn it with confidence, by level.

Heels

Strength, sensuality and attitude in heels. Choreography to help you shine and build confidence.

Not sure where to start? Message us and we'll recommend the ideal class for your level and taste.

Do you work shifts? Do you have a changing schedule? Do you travel, or simply can't always come on the same day?

Forget rigid monthly fees and losing money on days you can't make it: at Som Tribu we adapt to you.

Here you don't pay a monthly fee. With our passes, you decide when to come and how to spread out your classes.

Can't make it? No problem. Cancel at least 5 hours in advance and you won't lose that class — you can make it up another day.

⚠️ There are just three things to keep in mind:

  • ✅ Passes are valid for 2 months.
  • ✅ If you can't come, cancel your booking at least 5 hours beforehand to keep that class.
  • ✅ Passes are personal and non-transferable. Each person needs their own pass.

More freedom. More convenience. More dancing. At Som Tribu we adapt to your rhythm so the only thing you need to worry about is enjoying the dance.
